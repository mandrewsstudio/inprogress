In Progress / Parents for Future UK / Brand Identity

A bold, bright and brave identity for a growing movement of grown-ups.

Parents for Future is for all parents, not just activists.

The brand is an invitation to other parents to join the movement, and a message to those in power. Bold typography and a broad colour palette allows for unexpected pairings, and helps communicate along a spectrum of emotion from sober to urgent to joyful.
