We’re a small studio that creates big impact. Run by Mike and Lorna, In Progress specialises in design for nonprofits, grassroots campaigns and responsible companies.

Mike Andrews,
Creative Director

Mike spent over a decade in commercial branding before joining the Glimpse Collective in 2019, where he worked on campaigns, activations and brand identities with WWF, The National Trust, Patagonia, Choose Love and Oxfam, among others. He started In Progress in 2024 to bring top-tier design to progressive and grassroots groups that often don’t have the same resources as startups and big businesses.

Lorna Jones,
Operations Director

Lorna has helped nonprofits and businesses develop real, actionable sustainability and net-zero plans. With experience working with arts, culture, and climate organisations, she specialises in managing relationships and ensuring the design process is smooth, effective and collaborative.
